Here are the ingredients for Grilled Chicken:
  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Steps To Prepare

Here are the steps to prepare Grilled Chicken:
  • 1. Prepare Marinade:

    - In a bowl, mix olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, oregano, paprika, salt, and black pepper.

  • 2. Marinate Chicken:

    - Place the chicken breasts in a resealable plastic bag or shallow dish.

    - Pour the marinade over the chicken, ensuring all pieces are coated.

    - Marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes, or up to 8 hours for more flavor.

  • 3. Preheat Grill:

    - Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.

  • 4. Grill Chicken:

    - Remove the chicken from the marinade and place it on the grill.

    - Grill for 6-8 minutes on each side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C) and the

    chicken is cooked through.
  • 5. Rest and Serve:

    - Let the grilled chicken rest for a few minutes before serving to retain juices.
