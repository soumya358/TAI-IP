Masala Chai

Masala chai is a spiced tea from India, made by brewing black tea with aromatic spices and milk, creating a rich, flavorful beverage.

Here are the ingredients for masala chai:
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon loose black tea leaves
  • 1-2 tablespoons sugar (optional)
  • 2-3 slices of fresh ginger
  • 2-3 whole cloves
  • 2-3 whole green cardamom pods
  • 1 small cinnamon stick
  • Pinch of black peppercorns

Steps To Prepare

Here are the steps to prepare masala chai:
  • 1. Crush Spices:

    Using a mortar and pestle, lightly crush the ginger, cloves, cardamom pods, cinnamon stick, and black peppercorns to release their flavors.

  • 2. Boil Water:

    In a saucepan, bring water to a boil.

  • 3. Add Spices:

    Add the crushed spices to the boiling water and reduce heat to medium-low. Simmer for about 5 minutes to infuse the water with the spices.

  • 4. Add Tea Leaves:

    Add the black tea leaves to the saucepan and simmer for another 2 minutes.

  • 5. Add Milk:

    Pour in the milk and sugar (if using). Stir well and simmer for an additional 3-4 minutes.

  • 6. Strain and Serve:

    Strain the masala chai into cups and serve hot. Optional: sweeten with more sugar to taste.
