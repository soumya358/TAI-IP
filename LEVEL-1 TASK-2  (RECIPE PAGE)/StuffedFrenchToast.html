Stuffed French Toast

Stuffed French Toast is bread filled with sweet or savory ingredients, dipped in egg mixture, and fried until golden.

Here are the ingredients for Stuffed French Toast:
  • 8 slices of bread
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup cream cheese (softened)
  • 1/4 cup strawberry jam (or your favorite filling)
  • Butter (for frying)
  • Powdered sugar (optional, for serving)

Steps To Prepare

    Here are the steps to prepare Stuffed French Toast:
  1. 1. Prepare the Filling:

    - Spread cream cheese on four slices of bread.

    - Spread strawberry jam on the other four slices.

    - Press one cream cheese slice and one jam slice together to form a sandwich.

  2. 2. Make the Egg Mixture:

    - In a shallow bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, vanilla extract, and ground cinnamon.

  3. 3. Dip the Sandwiches:

    - Dip each sandwich into the egg mixture, making sure to coat both sides well.

  4. 4. Cook:

    - Heat a skillet or griddle over medium heat and melt a bit of butter.

    - Place the dipped sandwiches onto the skillet and cook until golden brown on both sides, about 2-3 minutes per side.

  5. 5. Serve:

    - Remove from heat and transfer to a plate.

    - Sprinkle with powdered sugar if desired, and serve warm.
