Omlete

An omelette is a quick, versatile dish made from beaten eggs, cooked, and often filled with various ingredients.

Here are the ingredients for an omelette:
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk or water
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil
  • Fillings (optional): cheese, vegetables, ham, mushrooms, herbs

Steps To Prepare

Here are the steps to prepare an omelette:
  • 1. Beat Eggs:

    - In a bowl, whisk together eggs, milk or water, salt, and pepper until well combined.

  • 2. Preheat Pan:

    - Heat butter or oil in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.

  • 3. Pour Egg Mixture:

    - Pour the egg mixture into the skillet.

  • 4. Cook Eggs:

    - As the eggs set, gently push them towards the center of the pan with a spatula, tilting the pan to

    let the uncooked eggs flow to the edges.
  • 5. Add Fillings (Optional):

    - When the omelette is almost set, add fillings such as cheese, vegetables, ham, or mushrooms to one

    side of the omelette.
  • 6. Fold Omelette:

    - Fold the omelette in half using a spatula.

  • 7. Serve:

    - Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve hot.
