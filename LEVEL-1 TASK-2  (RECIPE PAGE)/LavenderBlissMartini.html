Lavender Bliss Martini

A Lavender Bliss Martini is a sophisticated cocktail featuring the floral notes of lavender, mixed with vodka, elderflower liqueur, and lemon juice.

Here are the ingredients for a Lavender Bliss Martini:
  • 2 oz vodka
  • 1/2 oz elderflower liqueur (such as St-Germain)
  • 1/2 oz lavender syrup
  • 1/2 oz fresh lemon juice
  • Ice
  • Dried lavender buds for garnish (optional)

Steps To Prepare

Here are the steps to prepare a Lavender Bliss Martini:
  • 1. Prepare Glass:

    - Chill a martini glass by placing it in the freezer for about 10 minutes.

  • 2. Shake Ingredients:

    - Fill a cocktail shaker with ice.

    - Add vodka, elderflower liqueur, lavender syrup, and fresh lemon juice to the shaker.

    - Shake vigorously for about 15-20 seconds to mix and chill the ingredients.

  • 3. Strain and Serve:

    - Strain the mixture into the chilled martini glass.

  • 4. Garnish:

    - Optionally, garnish with a sprinkle of dried lavender buds on top.

  • 5. Serve:

    - Serve the Lavender Bliss Martini immediately and enjoy its floral and citrus flavors.
